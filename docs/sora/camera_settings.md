## üé• Camera Movement Tags (Square Brackets)

- **[Push in]** ‚Äî Camera moves toward the subject
- **[Pull out]** ‚Äî Camera moves away from the subject
- **[Pan left] / [Pan right]** ‚Äî Camera pivots horizontally (left/right)
- **[Tilt up] / [Tilt down]** ‚Äî Camera pivots vertically (up/down)
- **[Truck left] / [Truck right]** ‚Äî Camera physically slides left/right
- **[Pedestal up] / [Pedestal down]** ‚Äî Camera moves vertically up/down
- **[Zoom in] / [Zoom out]** ‚Äî Focal length changes (digital or lens zoom)
- **[Tracking shot]** ‚Äî Camera follows a moving subject
- **[Static shot]** ‚Äî Camera remains fixed
- **[Shake] / [Handheld]** ‚Äî Simulates organic, unsteady camera movement
- **[Arc]** ‚Äî Camera moves in a semicircular or curved path around subject
- **[Crane] / [Jib]** ‚Äî Sweeping vertical/horizontal arcs, often high-to-low or vice versa
- **[Steadicam]** ‚Äî Ultra-smooth stabilized movement
- **[Dolly]** ‚Äî Smooth forward/backward move (physically, not zoom)
- **[Whip pan]** ‚Äî Fast horizontal pan for dynamic transitions
- **[Roll]** ‚Äî Rotates the camera around its lens axis (Dutch angle)
- **[Bird‚Äôs Eye View]** ‚Äî Overhead top-down shot

## üî≤ Camera Shot Types & Framing

- **Close-Up (CU)** ‚Äî Face or detail, max intimacy
- **Medium Shot (MS)** ‚Äî Waist-up, balance detail and context
- **Wide Shot (WS)** ‚Äî Subject in full context/environment
- **High Angle Shot** ‚Äî Camera looks down on subject
- **Low Angle Shot** ‚Äî Camera looks up at subject
- **Bird‚Äôs Eye View** ‚Äî Directly overhead
- **Dutch Angle** ‚Äî Camera tilted for tension
- **Over-the-Shoulder (OTS)** ‚Äî Conversation/POV framing
- **Macro / Extreme Close-Up** ‚Äî Ultra-tight on detail (fabric, eye)
- **Three-Quarter Angle** ‚Äî Slightly turned for depth

## üé¨ Cinematic Techniques

- **Rack Focus / Depth-of-Field Pull** ‚Äî Shifts focus between subject planes
- **Slow Zoom** ‚Äî Gradual change of framing/emphasis
- **Handheld Sway** ‚Äî Organic, subtle movement
- **Circle Around** ‚Äî Full or partial orbit
- **Simulated Gimbal** ‚Äî Super-smooth tracking/follow
- **Freeze Final Frame** ‚Äî For seamless loop

## üì∑ Lens & Technical Settings
